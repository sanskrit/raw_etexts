Description = "Bundle RE31139, entitled \"stotrasaṅgrahaḥ\", comprises two main sections, combining ritual practice and devotional hymnology. The first section is devoted to Ātmārthapūjā and details preparatory and worship procedures, including Astrasandhi, Śivasandhi, and Śivasūryapūjā. It also provides the complete Ātmārthapūjā sequence, which reflects personal worship performed for one’s own spiritual benefit. The second section is a collection of stotras primarily dedicated to Lord Śiva. This includes Sadāśivāṣṭakam, Hālāsyanāthāṣṭakam, Liṅgāṣṭakam, Bhairavāṣṭakam, Śrīsthalīśvarāṣṭakam, Śivapañcākṣarastotram, Bhujaṅgapriyākalpakam, and Kṣetrapālavāṣṭakam, as well as several other hymns. These illustrate the rich devotional and theological dimensions of Śaiva worship.\n\nThe manuscript consists of 55 folios, each measuring approximately 150 mm × 303 mm and containing 6–12 lines of text. By combining practical ritual manuals with a wide range of Śaivastotra literature, this collection is an important source for understanding the liturgical procedures and devotional expressions central to the Śaiva tradition."
