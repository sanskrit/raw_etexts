Description = "RE31378 Astottarasatanamani. This bundle, entitled Devyxadaṣṭottaraśatanāmāvalī, is a compact yet extensive collection of Aṣṭottaraśatanāmāvalīs (lists of 108 sacred names) dedicated to a wide range of deities. The contents include Śivāṣṭottaraśatam, Vināyakāṣṭottaraśatam, Ṣoḍaśagaṇapatināmāni (the sixteen forms of Gaṇapati), Vallīdevasenāsameta Śrīsubrahmaṇyāṣṭottaraśatam, Devyaṣṭottaraśatam, Durgāparameśvaryaṣṭottaraśatam, Lakṣmyaṣṭottaraśatanāmāvalī, Sarasvatyaṣṭottaraśatam, Ramāṣṭottaraśatam, Rāmāṣṭottaraśatam, Kṛṣṇāṣṭottaraśatam, Narasimhāṣṭottaraśatam, Viṣṇvaṣṭottaraśatam, Hariharāṣṭottaraśatam, Saptarṣiṛṣyxadaṣṭottaraśatam, and Tripurasundaryxadaṣṭottaraśatam. The manuscript thus reflects a broad devotional spectrum encompassing Śaiva, Śākta, Vaiṣṇava, and Saura traditions.\n\nThe bundle consists of 43 folios, each measuring 11 cm × 1.5 cm, written in 7 lines per folio, arranged in 3–5 columns. Despite its small physical format, the manuscript preserves a rich liturgical collection intended for arcana and daily worship, making it an important source for understanding multi-deity devotional practices and name-recitation traditions."
