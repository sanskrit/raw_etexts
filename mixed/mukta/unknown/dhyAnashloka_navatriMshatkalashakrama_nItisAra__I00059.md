Description = "RE32583, Dhyānaśloka, Navatriṃśatkalaśakramaśca and Nītisāra. This bundle comprises three distinct sections that blend devotional, ritual, and ethical literature. The first section contains Dhyānaśloka, describing the forms (rūpa) of deities such as Gaṇapati, Subrahmaṇya, Sadāśiva, and others, intended for meditative visualization. The second part, Navatriṃśatkalaśakrama, explains the order of the rituals and ceremonies for the 39-kalaśa snapanapūjā.  The final section, Nītisāraśloka-s, presents over 100 verses on ethical conduct and moral instruction, reflecting traditional wisdom literature.\n\nThe manuscript consists of 29 folios, each measuring 35.56 cm × 4.20 cm and containing 6–9 lines per folio. This collection provides a special mix of meditation, rituals, and moral lessons, giving important understanding of the combined spiritual and practical aspects of the tradition."
