Bhaktotsavavidhi, 3. Varṣotsavavidhi, 4. Utsvava-prāyaścittavidhi, and 5. Agnivibhajanam. The chapters are discussed in detail, and occasionally rituals of Goddess Devī and bhakthas (souls that obtained self-realisation and unity with the Lord) are explained from the Lalitāgama and the ceremonial rituals of a year, including monthly, fortnight and full moon day rituals, are explained from Uttarakāmikāgama. The prāyaścittavidhi (act of amends for ritualistic errors) is explained as per the Kāraṇāgama and the ritual Agnivibhajanam performed with the nine divisions of fire (Lord Agni).\n\nThe bundle contains 39 folios, and the condition is a bit damaged.
