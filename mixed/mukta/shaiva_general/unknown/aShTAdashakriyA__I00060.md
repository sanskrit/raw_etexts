Description = "RE31059 Aṣṭādaśakriyā. This bundle focuses on specific elements of the eighteen ritual processes (kriyās) connected with pratiṣṭhā (consecration). It includes the Pratiṣṭhādhivāsanavidhi, which details the preliminary sanctification rites performed before installation. This is followed by sections on Tattvārchanā and Sparśāhuti, which elaborate on the ritual worship of tattvas. The final section includes partial sections of the Gaurīpratiṣṭhā detailing procedures for installing the goddess Gaurī; however, it appears incomplete.\n\nThe manuscript comprises 39 folios, each measuring 41.5 cm × 3.5 cm and containing 7–8 lines of text per folio. This bundle offers focused insight into specific ritual segments within the broader framework of pratiṣṭhā, making it a useful source for understanding the procedural depth of Śaiva consecration practices."
