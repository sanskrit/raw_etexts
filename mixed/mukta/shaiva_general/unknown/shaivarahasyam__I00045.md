Description = "RE32601, Saivarahasyam. The text entitled as stotrāṇi, the manuscript bundle is a valuable compilation of devotional hymns and the names dedicated to various deities. It includes Umāmaheśvarāṣṭottara from the Śivarahasya, Lalitāṣṭottaraśatam, Mīnākṣyaṣṭakam, Vighneśvarastotram from the Skāndapurāṇa, Indrākṣīstotram, Āditya-hṛdayam, Mārgasahāyāṣṭakam, Vighneśvarāṣṭottaranāmam, and rituals for Rudrapūjā. All together, these texts reflect a wide spectrum of stotra literature, spanning Śaiva, Śākta, and Sūrya traditions, serving both liturgical and devotional purposes.\n\nThe bundle consists of 55 folios, each measuring 210 mm in length and 35 mm in breadth, written in 6–7 lines per folio. The text is preserved in Sanskrit, recorded in both Grantha and Telugu scripts. Although the physical condition of the bundle is damaged and not well-preserved, it remains a significant witness to the transmission of multi-deity stotra traditions and their survival in diverse regional scripts."
