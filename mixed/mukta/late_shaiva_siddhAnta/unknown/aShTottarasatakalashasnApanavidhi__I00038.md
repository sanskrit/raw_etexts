Description = "RE30579 This manuscript bundle, titled Aṣṭottaraśatakalaśasnapanavidhi, is a compilation of diverse ritualistic and devotional texts. It begins with the Aṣṭottaraśatasnapanapaṭala from the Kāraṇāgamamahātantra, which details the procedure for offering ritual ablutions with 108 sacred substances. Following the above mentioned text, it also includes the Nāgapratiṣṭhāvidhipaṭala from the Pratiṣṭhā-tantra of Kāraṇāgamamahātantra, which outlines the consecration of serpent deities. Next, it also gathered the Aśvatthapratiṣṭhāvidhipaṭala from the Cintyāgamapratiṣṭhā-tantra describes the installation rites for the sacred Peepal tree (Aśvattha). Additionally, the text incorporates Pradoṣavratoddyāpana from the Māghamāhātmya part of Śrīpādmamahāpurāṇa, explaining the conclusion of the Pradoṣavrata. The manuscript concludes with the devotional hymn Bhairavāṣṭakam, a set of eight verses dedicated to Lord Bhairava.\n\nThe bundle contains 33 folios, and there are 7-8 lines in each folio. This manuscript offers a unique blend of ritual manuals, installation procedures, and devotional hymns, serving as a rich resource for scholars and practitioners of Śaivāgama and Purāṇic traditions."
