Description = "RE31372 - Kuṇḍasaṃskāravidhiḥ. The manuscript bundle deals with the ritual procedures for the consecration and performance of homa in various sacred fire-altars (kuṇḍas). The contents include sections on Āgneyakuṇḍam, Dakṣiṇakuṇḍa-homavidhiḥ, Nairṛtikuṇḍavidhiḥ, Uttarakuṇḍa-homavidhiḥ, Paścimakuṇḍa-homavidhiḥ, and Vāyukuṇḍa-homavidhiḥ, outlining the directional significance and ritual variations associated with each kuṇḍa. The manuscript appears to be incomplete, with the text breaking off before the full exposition of the rites.\n\nThe bundle comprises 12 folios, each measuring 16 cm × 1.5 cm, written in 7 lines per folio. Despite its fragmentary condition, the manuscript is an important source for understanding Śaivāgamic homa practices and the ritual of multi-directional kuṇḍa worship"
