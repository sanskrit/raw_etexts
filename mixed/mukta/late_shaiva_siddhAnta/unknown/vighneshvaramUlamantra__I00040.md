Description = "RE32409, This manuscript bundle, titled Vighneśvaramūlamantram, is a compilation of various rituals (Archanās) and hymns dedicated to multiple deities. It includes the Vighneśvaramūlamantram, followed by an extensive collection of Sahasranāmas (1000 names) and Aṣṭottaraśatanāmas (108 names) for deities such as Subrahmaṇya, Vallī, Devasenā, Sabhāpati, Śiva, Lalitā, Devī, Bhairava, and Vīrabhadra. The manuscript also contains significant texts like the Śrīvidyā Pañcadaśākṣarī Mahāmantram and Lalitā Triśati. These hymns and mantras serve as vital components of ritualistic worship and devotional practices.\n\nThe bundle contains 120 folios, and there are 6-7 lines and 3-5 columns in each folio. This manuscript provides a comprehensive guide to rituals (Archanā) for several deities, reflecting the diversity and richness of Śaiva and Śākta worship traditions. Its structured collection of mantras and hymns makes it an invaluable resource for practitioners and scholars of Hindu ritualistic literature."
