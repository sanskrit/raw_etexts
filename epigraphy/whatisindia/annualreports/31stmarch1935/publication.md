****PUBLICATION****

7. The final stitched proof of *South Indian Inscriptions* Vol. VIII was sent to the press during the year after receipt of the sanction for its strike-off from the Director-General of Archæology. One hundred and fifty fresh inscriptions were checked for the next volume of Tamil inscriptions with reference to their original impressions, and notes for about 50 of them drawn up. Nineteen villages were visited in this connection and the readings of 100 inscriptions which were either damaged or in an incomplete state were revised in situ. Of the Bombay-Karnatak Volume, pp. 1 to 61 of the second proof and pp. 91 to 134 of the first proof were checked with reference to the originals and their corrected proofs sent to the press. One hundred and four further pages of manuscripts were sent to the press for being set up in type.
