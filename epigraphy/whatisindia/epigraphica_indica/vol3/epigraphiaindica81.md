**TRANSLATION.  
  
*A.―Second Plate.***

....... . . . . . . . . . . . may the primeval glory, with four faces,¹⁴ the essence of the three Vêdas, promote your welfare !

......Of that Creator, **Marîchi** was the great mind-born son ; and of Marîchi’s body sprang the great sage **Kâśyapa.**

......Of the sage KÃ¢syÃ¢pa the resplendent **Sun** was the son, who dispels the darkness of all the worlds.

......Vivasvat’s¹⁵ son again was **Manu,** thoroughly familiar with the Vêdas and Vêdâṅgas, the first of the rulers of the earth, as *Ôṁ* is of the Vêdic texts.

......And Manu’s son was the wise **Ikshvâku,** devoted to him¹⁶ whose symbol is the Garuḍa ; he ruled the earth, equal to Âkhaṇḍala.¹⁷
