**POSTSCRIPT.**

......I take advantage of this opportunity to publish a short inscription of **Harihara II.** in Grantha characters, which is engraved at the entrance into the inner *prâkâra* of the **Kâmâkshî** temple at **Kâñchîpuram,** and which is dated in **Śaka-Saṁvat 1315** expired, the **Śrimukha** *saṁvatsara.*² It consists of a single Sanskṛit verse, which records that king Harihara (II.) provided a copper-door for the central shrine of the Kâmâkshî temple.
