**TEXT :**  
Agirakhitasa[^(\[1\])](parta23.html#_f%0Atn1) Bhojakaṭakasa suchi dānaṁ

**TRANSLATION:**  
A rail, the gift of Agirakhita *(Agnirakshita)*[^(\[2\])](parta23.html#_f%0Atn2), the Bhojakaṭaka (inhabitant of *Bhojakaṭa*).

Ramprasad Chanda first read the name of the donor correctly. Before him it was read *Atankhata* (Cunningham), *Atantata* (Hultzsch, Lüders) or *Atanata* (Barua-Sinha).
