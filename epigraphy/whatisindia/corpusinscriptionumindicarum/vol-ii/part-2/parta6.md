**PART A**

II\. *Non-religious Names*  
1. Appearance─colour, size, dress, voice, and parts of the body :  
(a) Male names─ \**Samaka*[^(\[1\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn1) *(śyāmaka)* A 66, \*A 73, \**Chula (Kshudra)*[^(\[2\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn2) A 51, *\*Chuladhaka (Kshudra?)* A 17, *Chulana (Kshudra?)* A 91, *Mahamukhi (Mahāmukhin)*[^(\[3\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn3) \*A 42, *Muḍa (Muṇḍa)* A 102, *Ghāṭila (Ghāṭa* ‘nape or back of the neck’?) A 28.  
(b) Female names─*Sāmā (Śyāmā)*[^(\[4\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn4) A 20, *Golā*[^(\[5\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn5) A 49, Ghosā *(Ghoshā)*[^(\[6\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn6) A 117, *Kachulā (Kañchulā* ‘a bodice’)[^(\[7\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn7) A 115.  
2. Mental dispositions and temperament :  
(a) Male names─*Anaṁda (Ānanda)* A 50, *Avisana (Avishaṇṇa)* A 82, A 83, *\*Naṁda (Nanda)*[^(\[8\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn8) A 69, \**Nadagiri*[^(\[9\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn9) *(Nandagiri)* A 54, *Naṁdagiri*[^(\[9\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn9) A 97, *Dhuta (Dhūrta* ‘ crafty, cheat’) A 96.  
(b) Female names─*Ujhikā (Ujjhikā* ‘one who has abandoned (?)’) A 114, *Nadutarā (Nandottarā)* A 119, \**Badhikā (Baddhikā* ‘one who is bound’) A 42.  
3. Wealth, fame, and birth :  
(a) Male names─ *Dhanabhūti*[^(\[10\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn10) A 1, A 2, *Vasuka*[^(\[11\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn11) A 46, *Seṭaka (Śreshṭhaka)*[^(\[12\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn12) A 18, *fātamita (fitāmitra ?)* A 26, *\*Apikinaka (Apigirṇaka ?)* A 67, *Yasika (Yaśas ?)*[^(\[13\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn13) A 136, *Gosāla = Tosāla (Gośāla* ‘born in a cow-stall’) A 90, *\*fāta*[^(\[14\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn14) A 56, \* *Paṁthaka (Panthaka* ‘bore on the way’?)[^(\[15\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn15) A 71, *Vijitaka* ‘one born in the country’ (?) A 104, *Suladha (Sulabdha)* A 22.  
(b) Female name─ *Avāsikā (Āvāsikā ‘*one who has a residence(?)’).  
4. Plants and animals :  
(a) Male name─*Atimuta (Atimukta)*[^(\[16\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn16) A 81, *Suga, Saga (Śuṅga)* A 1, A 2.  
(b) Female names─*Valimitā (Vellimitrā)* A 35, *Kujarā (Kuñjarā)*[^(\[17\])](parta6.html#_f%0Atn17) A 10.  
5. Unclassified male names :  
*fahira A 50, Yamiṭa* A 103.
