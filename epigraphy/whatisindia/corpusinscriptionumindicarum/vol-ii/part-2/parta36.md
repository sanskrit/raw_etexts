**PART A**

**3. A 55 DONATION BY A SCULPTOR (WITHOUT REFERENCE  
TO THE NATIVE PLACE)[^(\[1\])](parta36.html#_f%0Atn1)**

***A 55 (857)[^(\[2\])](parta36.html#_f%0Atn2) ; PLATE VIII***

EDITED by Cunningham, *StBh.* (1879), p. 141, No. 42, and Pl. LVI; Hultzsch, ɀDMG., Vol. XL (1886), p. 74, No. 135, and Pl., and IA., Vol. XXI (1892), p. 237, No. 135; Ramaprasad Chanda, *MASI.,* No. I (1919), p. 19, and Pl. V; Barua-Sinha, BI. (1926), p. 26, No. 92.

**TEXT :**  
Budharakhitasa rupakārakasa dānaṁ

**TRANSLATION :**

The gift of Budharakhita *(Buddharakshita),*[^(\[3\])](parta36.html#_f%0Atn3) the sculptor. The name *Budharakhita* is found as that of a monk in A 57 and A 58.
