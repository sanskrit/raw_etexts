**PART A**

**TEXT:  ** Na\[ṁ\]d\[i\]nagarikaya Idadevāya dānaṁ

**TRANSLATION:  ** The gift of Idadevā *(Indradevā)*[^(\[1\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn1), the Naṁdinagarikā (inhabitant of *Nandinagara*).

***A 46 (799); PLATE XXIV***

ON a pillar of the North-Eastern quadrant. Original lost. Edited by Cunningham, *StBh.* (1879), p. 138 No. 87, and Pl. LV; Hultzsch, *ɀDMG.,* Vol. XL (1886), p. 59, and IA., Vol. XXI (1892), p. 255 (refers only to the name of the place); Barua-Sinha, BI. (1926), p. 16, No. 41.

**TEXT:**

> > > > 1 Nāsika Gorakhitiya thabho dānaṁ  
> > > > 2 Vasukasa bhāriyaya^(\[)[^(2\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn2)

**TRANSLATION:**

The pillar (is) the gift of Gorakhitā *(Gorakshitā)*[^(\[3\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn3) from Nāsika; (of Gorakhitā) the wife of Vasuka.[^(\[4\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn4)

Paḍelakasa Pusakasa suchi dānaṁ[^(\[6\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn6)

TRANSLATION:  
The rail-bar (is) the gift of Pusaka *(Pushyaka),*[^(\[7\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn7) the Paḍelaka (inhabitant of *Paḍela).*[^(\[8\])](parta31.html#_f%0Atn8)
