**No. 352**

(*A. R. No*. 341 *of* 1930-31)

**SĪMANTŪRU, MANGALORE TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab (No. 3) set up in the Janārdanasvāmin temple

**1411 A.D.**

This record gives in detail the conditions of agreement reached between Chennaya Mēlaṇṭa *alias* Kunda-heggaḍe and his nephew on one side and Kinnika-sāmanta *alias* Bācha-heggaḍe and his nephew Kānta-heggaḍe on the other, in respect of the possession of some lands about which they had become estranged.

It is dated **Śaka 1333, Vikṛita, Mīna 13, Monday** corresponding to **1411 A.D., March 9**.
