**No. 243**

(*A. R. No.* 506 *of* 1928-29)

**KŌṬE, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the *prākāra* of the Mahāliṅgēśvara temple.

**\[Kulaśēkarāḷva\]**

****This record, which is in Kannaḍa language engraved in Malayālam characters, states that the king granted the taxes realised in Śivasahabra\[h\]maṇavūra (Śivasa-Brahmavūra?) to (the temple of) the god at Kōṭa for the maintanance of a hand-lamp (kai-veḷaku). Adambhaṭṭ- uḍeyarasa also appears to have participated in making the grant.

****The only details of the date given is that Jupiter was in Kumbha. The record is engraved in characters of about the 14th century.
