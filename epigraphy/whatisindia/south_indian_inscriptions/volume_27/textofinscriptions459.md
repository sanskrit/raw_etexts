**No. 346**

(*A. R. No*. 286 *of* 1931-32)

**HOSĀḶA, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Broken stone lying on a raised pial near the Bhaṇḍāri-kēri-maṭha

**Circa 14th century**

**** This fragmentary record seems to register a gift of land and money, for offerings and burning a perpetual lamp probably in a *maṭha* by Tirumakaṁbaḷi and Saṅkuseṭi The gift was entrusted to Raghuvara-tīrtha-śrīpāda. *Karaṅika* Mallarasa of Bārakūru wrote the record.
