**TEXT OF INSCRIPTIONS**

****It is dated **Śaka 807, \[Śōbhakrit\], Āshāḍha \[ba\] 13, Dakshiṇāyana**- \[**saṁkrānti**\]. The details are irregular. The cyclic year corresponded to Śaka 805 (**883 A.D**.) when the *saṅkrānti* occurred on Junee 24, Monday, the *tithi* being Āshāḍha-pūrṇimā. In Śaka 807 (885 A.D.) the *saṅkrānti* occurred on June 24, Thursday the *tithi* being Āshāḍha śu. 9. The former might be the intended date.
