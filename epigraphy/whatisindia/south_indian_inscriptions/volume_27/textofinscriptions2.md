**No. 3**

*(A. R. No.* 177 *of* 1931-32)

**BŌḌINĀYANIPALLE,PUNGANUR TALUK, CHITTOOR DISTRICT**

Hero-stone (No. 6) set up in a field to the west of the village

**Permāḍi (?). Circa 8th-9th century**

This record registers a gift of *Kalnāḍu* (land ?) by the king and the villagers, obviously to the family of **Rācha** of **Belatūru**, who died in a cattle-raid at Maṅgala. It refers to **Permāḍi** as ruling the kingdom and **Mayimana** **pallavareya** as governing **Puli-nāḍu**.
