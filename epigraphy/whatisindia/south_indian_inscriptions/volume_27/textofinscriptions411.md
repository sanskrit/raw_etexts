**No. 284**

(*A. R, No.* 351 *of* 1930-31)

**BANTRA, PUTTUR TALUK, SOUTH CANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the Mahāliṅgēśvara temple

**Nṛpamallarāja, Circa 9th century**

****This record, in characters of about the 9th century A. D., seems to record a political agreement entered into among four persons, viz., Narasiṅgan Durgarāja, son of Balle-oḍeya, Rāchamallan Durgarāja, brother of Viḷarittaḷiyarasa, the Kataṁba ruler whose name is not given and the king Nrpamallarāja himself, to the effect that they should discontinue mutual enmity and fights.
