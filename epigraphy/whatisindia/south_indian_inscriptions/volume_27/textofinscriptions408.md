**No. 279**

(*A. R. No*. 339 *of* 1940-41)

**PEDDAPASUPULA, JAMMALAMADUGU TALUK, CUDDAPAH DISTRICT**

Stone in the backyard (*doḍḍi*) of the house of Juvvimānu-pōchi-reḍḍi

**Circa 8th century**

****This damaged record refers to an attack on Nandakuru and mentions Bhūpaditya who is said to have been governing Rēnāḍu. The name of the king is lost. It records a gift of land.
