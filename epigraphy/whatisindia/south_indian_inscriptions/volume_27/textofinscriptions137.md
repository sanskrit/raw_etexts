**No. 94**

(*A. R. No*. 317 *of* 1931-32)

**KUTTŪRU, (hamlet of Hegguñje) UDIPI TALUK  
SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab standing in the Mahāliṅgēśvara temple

**Rāmachandra, 1422 A.D.**

This slightly damaged record registers a gift of land to god Mahādēva of a locality (name not clear) in Hegguñji by Dēvappa- guḍḍa, son of Chauḍaguḍḍa when **Haridēva-voḍeya** was governing **Bārakūru-rājya**.

It is dated **Śaka 1345 (current), Śubhakṛit, Chaitra ba. \[8, Tuesday**\] corresponding to **1422 A.D., April 14.**
