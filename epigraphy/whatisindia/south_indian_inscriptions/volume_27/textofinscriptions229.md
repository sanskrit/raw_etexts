**No. 143**

(*A. R. No.* 514 *of* 1928-29)

**SĀLIGRAMA, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the *prākāra* of the Narasiṁhamūrti temple

**Virūpāksha, 1469 A.D.**

**** This record is dated **Śaka 1390** (expired), **1391** (current), **Sarvadhāri, Chaitra śu. 15, Monday**. The Śaka year 1391 corresponds to the cyclic year Virōdhin and the details correspond to **1469, A.D., March 27.**

It states that when **Viṭhrasa-voḍeya** was administering **Bārakūru- rājya** under the orders of the king, the latter granted 250 *kāṭi-gadyāṇa* while Viṭhrasa-voḍeya granted 500 *kāṭi-gadyāṇa* amounting in all to 300 *ghaṭi-varāha-gadyāṇa* from out of the *siddhāya* tax due from the village Kōṭa which is described as the *bhaṇḍāra-sthala* of the royal place, at the request of the sixteen *adhivāsis*. Ten-thousand *Mahājagattu* and Voppuva-hegaḍe of Kōṭa, to the people of Kōṭa, for the merit of the king.
