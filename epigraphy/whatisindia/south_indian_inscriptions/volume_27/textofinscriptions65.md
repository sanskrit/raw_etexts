**No. 52**

(*A. R. No*. 297 *of* 1931-32)

**ACHLĀḌI, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up near the house of Patel Saṅkayya seṭṭi

**Bukka I, 1370 A.D.**

 This badly damaged record seems to register a gift of land to a deity at Achalāḍi when *Mahāpradhāna* **Gōparsa-voḍeya** was governing **Bārakūru-rājya**. The details are all lost.

It is dated **Śaka 1293, Sādhāraṇa, dvitīya Vaiśākha śu. 1, Vaḍḍavāra** corresponding to **1370 A.D., April 27**. The weekday was Saturday.
