**ĒṬŪRU, PANGANUR TALUK, CHITTOOR DISTRICT**

Hero-stone lying in a field about a mile to the north of the village

**Kannaradēva, circa 10th century**

This record refers to the death of Pṛithivīrāma-seṭṭi, son of Kāsera-Narilūra, in a cattle raid at Maṅgala when **Vajjaradēva** was administering **Pulināḍu**. Vikramāditya, son of Bijayitāchāri of Kōyatūr. who bore the epithet *bahu-guṇa-tējan,* wad the engraver of the record.
