**No. 295**

(*A. R. No.* 585 *of* 1929-30)

**PADU-ALEVUR, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the *prākāra* of Durgā-Paramēśvarī temple

**Circa 10th century**

****This record seems to register a gift of land for a perpetual lamp in the temple of a goddess (name not clear), by Obaliabbe of Kokkaraṇi, in the kingdom of Sāntaradēva.

****It is dated the cyclic year **Virōdhikṛit, Dhanus, Jupiter** in **Kuṁbha**.
