**KOḌAVŪRU (MALPE), UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in a field adjacent to the temple called Kallu-maṭha

**Ballamahādēvī, 1287 A.D.**

****This record registers the remission of taxes like *samudāya* and *beḍaṁgoḷu* collected upon the land belonging to Kṛishṇa Nāṇilitāya situated in Kuḍevūru *adhivāsa* of the village Śivaḷḷi, by the Queen when she was giving audience in the palace at **Bārahakanyāpura**, in the presence of all the *prādhāna*, dēśi-*purusha* and *bāhattaraniyōgi*.

****It is dated Śaka **1210, Sarvajit, Tulā (Tula) 24, Wednesday** corresponding to **1287 A.D., October 24.**
