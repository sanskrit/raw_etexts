**No. 76**

(*A. R. No*. 232 *of* 1931-32)

**GUṆḌUGALLU, PUNGANUR TALUK, CHITTOOR DISTRICT**

Base of *dhvaja-staṁbha* in a betel-garden called *Kambadagaḍḍa-maḍi*

**Harihara II, 1400 A.D.**

****This is dated **Śaka 1322, Vi\[krama\], Prathama-Chaitra ba. 8, Wednesday** corresponding to **1400 A.D., March 17, Wednesday, f.d.t. .54.**

****It states that seven goldsmiths of Guṁḍuṁgalu whose names are given and who were sons of Kandōja, had the lamp-pillar erected, to god Mallikārjunadēva of Śrīparvata for the merit of their parents during the period of Mallinātha, younger brother of Dēvarāya who was an agent of Bhikshāvṛitti-Siddayya and Dēvayya. Guṁḍuṁgalapura is stated to have belonged to the temple of the deity. Veṅkayya wrote the record.
