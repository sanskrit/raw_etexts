**No. 58**

(*A. R. No*. 489 *of* 1928-29)

**UPPŪRU, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab lying near a well about two furlongs from the Vināyaka temple

**Bukka I**

This badly damaged record seems to record a gift of land, after purchase, to Narasiṁhadēva-ayya on the occasion of a **solar eclipse**, The details are all lost. **Malaya-daṇṇāyaka** is stated to have been governing **Bārakūru-rājya.** The record refers to Upūru.
