**TEXT OF INSCRIPTIONS**

**No. 93**

(*A. R. No*. 545 *of* 1929-30)

**PADUVARI, COONDAPOOR TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the field about two furlongs to the south east of the  
Roman-catholic church

**Dēvarāya I, 1419 A.D.**

 This record, with the latter portion of which being completely damaged, registers a gift of land in Hariharapura of Chikkanakēri made after purchase from the *nāḍu* and *naḍagi* of Bayidūr, by the *seṭṭikāra* of Bayidūr, to Viṭhappa and Māyaṇṇa, sons of Mallappa of Gautam-gōtra, probably on behalf god Senēśvara.

It is dated **Śaka 1344 (current), Plava, Kārttika śu. 1, Monday** corresponding to **1421 A.D., October 27**.
