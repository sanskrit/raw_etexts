**No. 71**

(*A. R. No*. 350 *of* 1934-35)

**HAMPI, HOSPET TALUK, BELLARY DISTRICT**

Rock behind the Prasanna-Virūpāksha temble in Hēmakūṭa

**Harihara II, 1397 A.D.**

This records the construction of a lamp pillar (*dīpamāleya- kaṁbha*) in the temple of Jaḍeya-Śaṅkaradēva by the queen Bukkayave for the merit of her preceptor Bhāskaradēvayya.

It is dated in the cyclic year **Īśvara, Mārgaśira Paurṇami, Tuesday**. The given cyclic year falls in the reign-period of Harihara II when the details regularly correspond with **1397 A.D., December 4**.
