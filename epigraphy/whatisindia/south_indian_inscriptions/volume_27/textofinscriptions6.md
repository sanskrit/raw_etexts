**No. 8**

(*A. R. No.* 235 *of* 1937-38)

**KAMALĀPURAM, KAMALAPURAM TALUK, CUDDAPAH DISTRICT**

Nandi-pillar set up in front of the Pātāḷa-Sōmēśvarasvāmin temple

**Indra (III), 925 A.D.**

This damaged record is dated **Śaka 848, Pārthiva, Uttarāyaṇa- saṁkrānti.** The detailes given are insufficient for verification. However, the *saṁkrānti*, occurred on **December 23,** the year corresponding to **925 A.D.** The Śaka year was current.

It seems to record the construction of the door-way and the *maṇḍapa*, and the repair of the shrines for the eight *Parivāra-dēvatas* of the Mulkiṁṭīśvara temple, by a *Mahāsāmanta* whose name cannot be made out, when Pallava-dhīra was governing **Muḷki-500** division. the record states that the king, **Indra III,**was ruling the *mahāraṭṭa rājya*.
