**No. 78**

(*A. R. No*. 486 *of* 1928-29)

**CHĀNTĀU, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab set up in the prākāra of the Śaṅkaranārāyaṇa-svāmin temple

**Harihara, 1404 A.D.**

This record is dated **Śaka 1\[3\]25, Svabhānu, Phālguṇa śu, 1, Sunday.** The **tithi** corresponds to **1404 A.D., February 12** when the weekday was **Tuesday**.

It states that Śaṅkarāraṇya-śrīpāda, disciple of Vidyāraṇya-śrīpāda obtained a gift of land from Kuṁjūraṇṇa-uḷḷūra, Jātaṇṇa-upādhya, and Īśvara-uḷḷūra, and made it over to the temple of Śaṇkaranārāyṇa, of Niḍuvaṁpaḷḷi, in the village of Brahmūru when **Mahābaḷadēva-voḍeya** was governing **Bārakūru**.
