**No. 73**

(*A. R. No*. 359 *of* 1930-31)

**KANYANA, COONDAPOOR TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab (No. 3) set up in Gubbukōṇe Gōpālakṛishṇa temple

**Harihara II, 1398 A.D.**

****This is dated **Śaka 1320, Īsvara, Phālguṇa ba. 10, Thursday** corresponding to **1398 A.D., March 13**. The weekday however, was Wednesday.

****It registers a sale deed of land made under certain conditions to Liṅgarasa by the six *jananis*, fifty of *Jagattu* and others Rājāḍi Kanyāna.
