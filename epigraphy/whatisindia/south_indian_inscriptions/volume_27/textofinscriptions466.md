**No. 353**

(*A. R. No.* 335 *of* 1930-31)

INUVALLI, MANGALORE TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT

Two broken slabs lying in the Durgātemple

1413 A.D.

****This badly damaged record is dated **Śaka \[1\]335, Vijaya, \[Śrāvaṇa\] 1**, corresponding to **1413, A. D., July 30.** The details are not verifiable.

****It seems to register gifts of income to a deity and of oil for burning a lamp in the temple in the month of Vṛiśchika. It refers to the village Yinavaḷi. It is fragmentary and details are lost.
