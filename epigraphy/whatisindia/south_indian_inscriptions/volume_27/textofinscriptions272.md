**No. 163**

(*A. R. No*. 276 *of* 1931-32)

**HOSĀḶA. UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab lying in the garden adjoining the tank at Chauḷikēri

**Kṛishṇadēvarāya, 1514 A.D.**

****This right side of the slab bearing this record is completely damaged and chipped off. It registers a gift of land to a deity and for feeding some Brāhmaṇas, It refers to Lakshmīnārāyaṇa, probably the deity, and mentions that **Vijaya** . . . ., son of a certain **Voḍeya** was governing **Bārakūru-rājya**.

****It is dated **Śaka 143\[7\], Bhāva, Chaitra śu. 1, Monday** corresponding to **1514 A.D., March 27**. The Śaka year was current.
