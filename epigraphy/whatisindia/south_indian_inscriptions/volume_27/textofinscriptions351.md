**No. 214**

(*A. R. No*. 491 *of* 1928-29)

**NĪLĀVARA, UDIPI TALUK, SOUTH KANARA DISTRICT**

Slab (No. 2) set up in the *prākāra* of Mahishāsuramardinī temple

**Ballamahādēvī, 1277 A.D.**

****This indifferently engraved record registers a gift of money and land to goddess Bhagavatī of Niruvāra by the *sama\[sta\] pradhāna, dēśapurusha* and other officials of this village.

****It is dated **Śaka 1201, Īśvara, Kanyā 15, Sunday**. The cyclic year Īśvara corresponds to Śaka 1199 when the other details regularly corresponds to **1277 A.D., September 12**.
