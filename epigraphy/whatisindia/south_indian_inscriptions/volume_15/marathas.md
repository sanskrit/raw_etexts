**BOMBAY-KARNATAKA INSCRIPTIONS**

**THE MARATHAS**



**No.270**

**(*B.K. No.* 218 of 1928-29)**

**BHADRAPUR, NAVALGUND TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

**On a slab set up near the village *chavadi***

**Sivaji, 1679 A.D.**

This is dated Saka 1601, Siddharthi, Sravana su 4, corresponding to 1679 A.D., July 31 (not verifiable).

It records the grant of the privileges of *Gaudike* and *Sanabovike* to Samgappa. It mentions the temples of Hanamanta and Sankaralinga. The record is damaged and indifferently engraved.
