**No.153**

**(*B.K. No.* 23 o*f* 1930-31)**

**MANAGOLI, BAGEVADI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

On a slab built into the wall right of entrance of the *mandapa* of the Hanuman temple

**Jatiugi, Circa 1200 A.D.**

The date portion of the inscription is lost. In the introductory portion are mentioned an officer of the king, named Sahadeva and his elder brother *Dandesa* Mallideva. The stone is broken and all other are lost.

Published in the *Epigraphica. Indica,* Vol. V, Page 28 ff.
