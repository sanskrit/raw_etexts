**BOMBAY-KARNATAKA INSCRIPTIONS**

**THE VIJAYANAGARA KINGS**

***Harihara II***

**No.244**

**(*B.K.No.* 223 of 1926-27)**

**ABBIGERI, RON TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

On a slab built into the front wall of the house of Guracharya Panchamukhi

**Harihara II, 1379 A.D.**

This is dated Saka 1301, Siddharthi, Jyestha su. 15, Tuesday, lunar eclipse, corresponding to 1379 A.D., May 31.

It records a gift ofland in Avvegeri to Hemadriyacharya of Yarigeri by the king. The record is in Nagari characters, the language being Kannada.

**No.245**

**(*B.K.No.* 37-*A* of 1927-28)**

**KALAKAPPANA-GUDDA, RON TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

On a stone lying against the wall at the left of the entrance into the central shrine of Kalakalesvara temple

**\[Barihara II\]**

This worn out epigraph is engraved below No.165 above. It mentions Bomma\[gauda\].

***Devaraya II***

**No.246**

**(*B.K.No.* 3 of 1927-28)**

**SAVADI, RON TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

On a pillar in the Brahmadeva temple

|     |
|-----|
| \>  |

**\[Devaraja II\], 1431 A.D.**

This is dated the cyclic year Virdohi, Pushya su.15, the Saka year being lost. The name of the king, who might be Devaraja II, is not fully preserved and the cyclic year may be a mistake for Virdohikrit. In that case the date of the inscription would be 1431 A.D., December 19.

It records the renovation of the temple of Traimurti at Saividi in Badavi-sime by Chavudaya-nayaka, son of Tipana-nayaka of Bagilavada.

**No.247**

**(B.K.No. 117 of 1929-30)**

**BAILUR, HONAWAR TALUK,M NORTH KANARA DISTRICT**

On a slab standing near the Haslara huts

**Devaraya**

II, 1433 A.D.

This is dated Saka 1356, Pramadi, Karttika ba.10, the weekday being lost. This corresponds to 1433 A.D., November 6. The Saka year was current.

It records an endowment made in favour of Narayanabhatta of Gujjadi, son of Kesava, by *Adhikari* Sanne Nayaru in conjuction with the pontiff Suresvara-Sripada and the people of the Bailur region for the exposition of the *Puranas* every day in the temple of madakesavara and also in the *matha* of the said teacher for thirty days in the month of Sone (Sravana).

**No.248**

**(B.K.No. 118 of 1929-30)**

**BAILUR, HONAWAR TALUK,M NORTH KANARA DISTRICT**

On a broken slab lying in the Markandesvara temple

**Devaraya II, 1433 A.D.**

This is dated Saka 1356, Pramadi, Karttika ba. 10, Sunday. The details corresponds to 1433 A.D, November 6, the Saka year being current. The weekday, however, was Friday.

The contents of the record are the same as in No.247 above. This seems to be almost a copy of it.
