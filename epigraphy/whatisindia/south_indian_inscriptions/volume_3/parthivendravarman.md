This record is dated in the 3^(rd) year and 173^(rd) day of Parthivendradhipativarman who took the head of the Pandya (*king*).
Here again the assembly of Uttarameru-chaturvedimangalam declared certain lands tax-free in favour of a temple after receiving *purvacharam* from Sandiran Elunurruvan *alias* Nulamba-Mayilattiyar, a resident of Kandapurattupettai.
