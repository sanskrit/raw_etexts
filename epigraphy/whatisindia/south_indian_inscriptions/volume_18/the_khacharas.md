**The Khacharas of Basavur**

Though a minor family, the Khacharas occupied a prominent position exercising authority over the division of Basavur Hundred-and-forty, of over a century. It is not possible however to give a connected account or genealogy of these local dignitaries and therefore it may be useful to give a list of their names in chronological order.
