**(B.K.No.84 OF 1936-37)**

**YARGAL(BHUJRUK), SINDGI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Hero- stone set up in the Hanuman temple A.D. 777(?)**

This record is dated **saka \[7\]00, Durmati, chaitra, Amavasya, \[satur\]day. If the** saka year was current, the details given corresponding to **A.D. 777, April 12,** when the cyclic year was Pingala and not Durmati. In the cyclic year Durmati corresponding to saka 703 (781 A.D ), the *tithi* fell on March 29, Thursday.

This badly damaged inscription record the death of an individual in a fight it refers to Kanna and a *perggade.*
