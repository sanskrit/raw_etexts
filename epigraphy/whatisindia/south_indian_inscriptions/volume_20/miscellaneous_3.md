**MISCELLANEOUS INSCRIPTIONS**

**No. 381**

(Page No 377)

**(B.K.No.10 of 1937-38)**

**BABLAD, INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Stone built into the platform in front of the Hanuman temple A.D.1440**

This badly damaged and effaced record is dated saka 1362, raudri, Bhadrapada, su. 11, Tuesday, corresponding to A.D.140, September 6, Tuesday, f.d.t. 92.

The inscription seems to record a gift of land by certain individuals.



**No.382**

(Page No 378)

**(B.K.No.48 of 1938-39)**

**ALGUR, JAMKHANDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT **

**Pedestal of the image of Adinatha- tirthankara**

A.D. 1445

This damaged record is dated saka \[13\] 66, raktashi, Magha, bu., Sunday, the tithi being not given. The intended date may be A.D.1445, January 24 or 31, Sunday

The inscription seems to record the installation of the image of Adinatha.



**No. 383**

(Page No 378)

**(B. K. No. 70 of 1938-39)**

**SATULGAV, INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Top of the door frame of Hanuman temple**

A.D.1481

This record is dated **saka1\[40\]3, piava, Margasira, su, \[7\], Saturday,** corresponding to **A.D.1481, November 27,** the weekday being Tuesday and not Saturday.

This inscription records the completion of a hall (sabha) evidently in the temple of hanuman. It mentions Ramarasa who perhaps was responsible for this act.

**No. 385**

(Page No 379)

**(B.K.No.97 of1935-36)**

**SIDENUR, HIREKERUR TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

**Broken slab lying in survey No.2**

15^(th) century A.D.

This record, in characters of about the 15th century A.D., is dated the cyclic year Raudra\[puspha,su\]5, Tuesday. The details cannot be verified. It record the death of Mallaya, son of Bhayiraya-nayaka, and younger brother Machaya.
