This record is dated in **Saka 1109, kingâ€™s regnal year 2, Plavanga, Karttika,**

The inscription records a gift of land by Rechi-bhatta of Kamme-vamsa, one of

The Three Hundred *Mahajanas* of **Kalkere** included in Hagaritaga-nadu, after

Purchasing it from **Vikramanaka-dandadhipa,** the *prabhu of **Asmatataka** (I, e., Kalkere),* for the feeding house *(satra)* of the place when Mahamandalesvara **Viunadevarasa was** administering **Hagaritage-nadu.** The gift was made with the consent of the *Mahaianas* of Kalkere. The donor Rechibhatta is stated to be the son of

Padmanabha-bhattopadhyaya and grandson of Nandyanavedi.

The record refers to the regnal year of Vira-Narayana who is identified with the

Yadava king Bhillama on the ground that the Mahamandalesvara Viruparasa and

Vikramanka dandadhipa are mentioned in other of this king.¹

No. 175

(Page No. 218)

**( B. K. No. 36 of 1937-38)**

**HIRE BEVINUR,INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

### **Slab set up near the temple of Bhogalinga**

Bhillama-A. D. 1190

This record is dated **Saka 1112** (*nara-netra-Sasi-Soma-Indu),* **Sadharana,**

Sranvana, Purnima, Thursday,

corresponding to **A. D. 1190, July 18.** The week-day was Wednesday, when the *tithi* ended at 72.

The inscription states that at the request of Siridevi, the wife of Boppana-nayaka,

To observe *kshitiruha-nompi,* Dandanayaka **Mayideva,** the minister of the king,

Planted a number of trees and established a charitu fair *(dharma-santhe)* in

**Beviner** situated in **Tardavadi 1000,** in the presence of *Mummuridandas* and other of

Thirty-six-villages division. Boppana-nayaka was governing the division of Thirty-six village as a subordinate of Mayideva.

It also records a gift of land and house for the maintenance of the above

Charties made to Bandarasa, the *manneya* of Tarddavadi-1000 and to Boppana-

Nayaka by Mayideva. Further gifts of trees to the temples of Taleyakavadeva,

Revanesvara and Bhogesvara and also to Somanatha-pandita, the composer of the

Record, are registered.
