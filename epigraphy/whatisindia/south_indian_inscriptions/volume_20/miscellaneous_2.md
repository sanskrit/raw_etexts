**No. 343**

(Page No 359)

**(B.K.No.34 of 1937-38)**

**HIRE-BEVINUR, INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Stone standing near the siddaramesvara temple 13th century A.D.**

This inscription, in characters of about the **13^(th) century A.D.,** records the imprecation against the misappropriation of the gifts(?) made to the temple of Bhoganathya, Revanasiddha, Maliikarjuna, teleyakava-ramesvara, Brahmesvara and Mailaradeva.

**No. 344**

(Page No 359)

**(B. K. No. 45 of 1937-38)**

**NADA(KHURD), INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT **

**stone built into the front wall of the Hanumantadeva temple 13th century A.D.**

This record, in characters of about the **13th century A.D.,** states that the stone indicates the boundary of the field to the west of the village Arjunige and east of the village Nada.

**No. 345**

(Page No 360)

**(B. K. No. 50 of 1937-38)**

**NIMBAL, INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Fragment of stone built into the wall (inner-side), left of entrance into the**

**Ranga mandapa of the Sankaralinga temple 13th Century A.D.**

This record,in characters of about the **13^(th) Century A.D.,** seems to register a gift of toll incomes on certain articles of merchandise.

**No. 346**

(Page No 360)

**(B. K. No. 54 of 1937-38)**

**NIMBAL, INDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Right face of a fragment of stone lying in the**

**compound of Sankaralinga temple 13th Century A.D.**

This fragmentary record, in characters of about the **13^(th) Century A.D.,** registers a gift of land and house-site to Barmayya, by Somayya, devotee of Kotisankaradeva, for the maintenance of a charity picotta.
