## **No.154(Page No 196)**

**(B. K. No.42 of 1936-37)**

**KADLEVAD, SINDGI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

### **Slab set up in the temple of Sivalinga**

Sovideva---A. D.1172

This record is dated Saka 1094, Nandana, Pushya, su. 8, Monday, Uttarayana- Samkranti, corresponding to A. D. 1172, December 24. The week-day was, however Sunday.

The inscription states that Dandanayaka Kumara Bammidevarasa who was Administering Sagara, Hagarittage and Elamela divisions and Mahamanadalesvara Simgidevarasa of Elamele visited the temple of Svayambhn Somanathadeva at the Ghatikasthana Kadalevada and made a gift of land for worship, offering and the feeding Of the ascetics in the temple. The gift was entrusted to acharya Sovarasi-panditadeva Who, in his turn, made some gift of land to his son (Putra) Samgoja who is called an architect (sutradhari) and an export in building.

The king is stated to have been ruling from Seleyahalli.
