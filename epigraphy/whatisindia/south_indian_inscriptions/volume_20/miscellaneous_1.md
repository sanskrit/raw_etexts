**No. 302**

(Page No 336)

**(B. K. No. 60 of 1936-37)**

**KOADGULI, SINDI TALUK, BIJAPUR DISTRICT**

**Broken slab fixed into the right outer wall of Hanuman temple**

**12th century A.D.**

This fragmentary inscription record a gift of land measured by the Bhanduchintamani pole, house-site, garden, etc., to a god and two hundred vajasaneya Brahmanas of the place. All others details are lost. In characters of about the 12th century A.D.
