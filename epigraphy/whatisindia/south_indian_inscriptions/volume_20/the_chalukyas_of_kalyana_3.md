**KAMADHENU, KALGHATGI TALUK, DHARWAR DISTRICT**

### **Slab lying in front of the Kallesvara temple **

### Bhulokamalla (Somesvara III)â€”A.D. 1129

This record is dated Chalukya-Vikrama year 54, Saumya, Chaitra su. 2, Monday, Samkranti. The tithi corresponds to A.D. 1129, March 23 which was Saturday. The Mesha-samkranti occurred on the next day. 

The inscription records a gift of land and money to god Kalidevasvami of Sasalu By Mallisetti of Hulivara when Mahamandalesvara Jayakesideva of the Kadamba Family was governing Komkana-900 and Palasige-12000 divisions. The gift land Which was purchased by the donor from Somesvara-bhatta, the son of Narayana- Bhatta, the urodeya of Sasalu, and two others, was entrusted into the hands of Tejorasi-pandita, the disciple of Devarasi-pandita, the acharya of the temple. The Record gives a genealogical account of the donor.****
