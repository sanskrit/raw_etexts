**Inscriptions of the Ranganathasvamy Temple, Srirangam**

**Cholas**



**Kulottunga I**

**No. 26**

(Page No. 21)

**(A. R. No. 56 of 1938-39)**

**III Prakara, south wall.**

Records, in Sanskrit written in grantha chatracters that **Vanadhiraja**, minister of **Jayadhara** i.e. **Kulottunga** raised a prakara in this temple. (?)

**No. 27**

(Page No. 21)

**(A. R. No. 112 of 1932-39)**

**III prakara, east wall.**

Much damaged. Begins with the introduction *Pugal sulnda punari* of the king. Registers gift of land **Arumoli Rajadhirajan** alias **Vanadhirajar** for rearing a flower garden. 

**No. 28**

(Page No. 23)

**(A. R. No. 109 of 1947-48)**

**III Prakara, north wall.**

Seems to record a gift of land in Vilattur-nadu to **Chola-Keralanallur Udaiyan Adittan Vedavanamudaiyan** *alias* **Neriyan Muvendavelar** for a flower garden (tirunandavanappuram) for supplying two hundred garlands and some specified quantity of paddy to the temple at the instance of **Nishadarajar** the Srikaryam officer of the temple. 

**No. 29**

(Page No. 26)

**(A. R. No. 110 of 1947-48)**

**III Prakara, north wall.**

Partly built in. Records an assignment by sale of fallow land for payment of 10 *kalam* of paddy to the temple by the temple executive towards the expenses of two festivals to be conducted every year in the temple. The details of the recipients of the land are lost.

**No. 30**

(Page No. 27)

**(A. R. No. 117 of 1947-48)**

**III Prakara, north wall.**

Records a sale of *devadana* land in Kil-Pilaru in Pachchil-kurram to Kasyapan Kannan Narayanan *alias* Vaisravana Bhattar of Tiruvellarai , a *Brahmadeya* in Vadavali-nadu in Ulagamulududaiyal-nadu. Another similar transactions by the temple executives to **Alagiyaraja Muvendavelar** is referred to in lines 9-11 of the inscriptions. The land given seems to have been named *Gangaiyarsurabhi-vilagam*.

**No. 31**

(Page No. 28)

**(A. R. No. 131 of 1947-48)**

**III Prakara, west wall.**

Registers a sale of land by the temple executives to Ponnambalakkuttan *alias* Kalingarayar.

**No. 32**

(Page No. 21)

**(A. R. No. 136 of 1947-48)**

**III Prakara, north wall.**

Registers a gift of land by sale to **Sendamangalamudaiyan Jayangondsolan** *alias* **Senapati Ilangovelar** for a garden to be named *Kidarangonda-vilagam*.
