**VIJAYARAJENDRA**

**No. 114 (Page No 100)**

**(A. R. No. 114 Of 1906)**

**South Arcot District, Tirukkoyilur Taluk, Jambai**

**Jambunatha Temple-In The Same Place**

**Tribha. Vijayarajendradeva (Kulottunga I?)-4^(th) Year**

This records the construction of the entrance and the gopura of the temple of tantonri-Aludaiyanayanar at Sanbai alias Virarajendrasolapuram in Vanagoppadi by Valavan Pallavaraiyan Mandaippillai, Vijayarayan, the headman of Kurugadi in Tenkila-nadu, a division of Nittavinoda-valanadu (See No. 99 above).
