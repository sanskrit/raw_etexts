**JATAVARMAN SUNDARAPANDYA**

**No. 5 (Page No. 2)  
(A. R. NO. 5 of 1906)  
Salem District, Namakkal Taluk, Namakkal  
Deserted Temple On The Hill-On The North West And South Walls  
Jat. Sundara-Pandya-2nd Year.**

This registers an endowment of wet lands 11 kani in extent, in different places for the requirements of food offerings and lamp in the temple of
Arulala-Vinnagara alias Edirilipperumal on the hill at Tiruvaraikkal, by the *Sabha* of Elur-nadu and the Nattar and the Nagarattar.
