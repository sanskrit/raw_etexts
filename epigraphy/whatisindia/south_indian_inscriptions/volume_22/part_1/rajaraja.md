**INSCRIPTIONS COLLECTED DURING THE YEAR 1905-1906**

**RAJARAJA**

**No. 134** **(Page No 109)**

**(A. R. No. 134 Of 1906)**

**South Arcot District, Tirukkoyilur Taluk, Elvanasur**

**Gramardhanatha Temple-On The Same Wall**

**Rajarajadeva-14^(th) Year.**

This records a gift of 16 *kasu* of burning a light during the evening service in the temple of Urbagankondaruliya-Nayanar, by Mangalankilan Aimbolil-Raman of Mandaiyur in Tirumunaippadi-nadu. This was deposited with a Sivabrahmana of the temple.

**No. 138** **(Page No 113)**

**(A. R. No. 138 Of 1906)**

**South Arcot District, Tirukkoyilur Taluk, Elvanasur**

**Gramardhanatha Temple-On The Same Wall**

**Tribh. Rajarajadeva-10th Year.**

This records a gift of 32 cows to the temple of Urbagankondarulina-Mahadeva at Iraiyanaraiyur alias Solakerala-chaturvedimangalam, by Mudigondan Kari alias Virapalan a resident of Vanapiratti village in Vadanaraiyur kurram for the merit of a certain Tillai alias Arayirappoyan whom he had hit with an arrow by accident and who died as a consequence.
