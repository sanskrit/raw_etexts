SOUTH INDIAN INSCRIPTIONS

VOLUME XVII

**INSCRIPTIONS COLLECTED DURING 1903 - 1904**

**TONDAMAN**

**Ramachandra Tondaiman**

**No. 386.**

**(A. R. No. 356-A of 1904).**

**Kudumiyamalai, Kulattur Taluk, Tiruchirappalli District.**

**On the south wall of the Akhilandesvari shrine in the**

**Sikhanathasvamin temple.** **Ramachandra Tondaiman S. 1787 : 1866 A.D.**



The details of date viz., Saka 1787, Kali 4966, Krodhana, Tai 29, Friday, Anuradha correspond to 1866 A.D. February 9, Friday.

It records that the Kumbhabhisheka of the temple of god Sikhanathasvamin and the goddess Akhilandesvari was performed on the said date.
