SrI jayarAyara stOtra suLAdi-dRuvatALa-vijayaviThala

--------------------------------------------------

jayarAya jayarAya jayadEvi arasannAshrayamADi koMDippa

tappO vittapa|

bhayava pariharisi bhavadurara haribha|

ktiyakoDu j~nAna vairAgyadoDane|

daya dRuShTiyiMda nODu kApADu mAtADu|

laya vivarjitavAda vaikuMTake|

sayamAgi mArga tOro sajjanaroLagiTTu|

jayava pAlisu enage yatikula ranna|

AyuttakkAdaru nAnu aihika saukhyavanolle|

bayasuva satatadalli hariyanAma |

trayagaLe nAligeli nenedu karma|

kShayavAguvaMte kShipradalli biDide|

prayatnaveMbOdidake sArthakavAgali|

payOnidhi suteramaNa manadoLu nillalu|

payaH pAnadiMdadhika nimma darushana venage|

priya mattoMdAvudu illa idE balu lAbha|

gaya kAshi trisaMgama modalAda tIrtha kShEtra

nayadiMda mADida phalabappodu|

payOdharagaLa tinadaMte mALpudu|

hRudayadoLagippa danenge idE EkAMta|

bhayakRudbhayanAsha vijaya viThalana sEveya mALpa

sadguNashIla sujana pAlA ||

maTTatALa

---------

Avajanumada puNya phalisito enageMdu|

rAvutarAgidda jayatIrthara kaMDe |

dEvAMsharu ivara svarUpanvannu |

bhAvadiMdali tiLidu koMDADuvanbaludhanya|

pAvina pariyalli illi irutippa|

kAvuta bhakutarana pAvana gaisuva|

dEva dEvEsha siri vijaya viThalanaMghri|

tAvarebhajisuve niShkAmuka mauni|

triviDi tALa

------------

vaiShNava janma baMdudakidE sAdhana |

viShNuvina bhakuti dorikidudake|

naShTavAyitu enna saMcitagAmikarma|

kaShTadAridryagaLu hiMdAdavU tRuShNanAdeno enna

kulakOTi sahita A|

ariShTa mArgake innu pOge nAnu|

sRuShTiyoLage ivara darshanavAgadale|

spaShTavAda ~g~jAna puTTidayyA|

iShTukAla biDade muMde mADuva balu|

niShTage anukUla tAtvikaru|

SiShTAcAravannu mIridale nimma|

iShTArtha bayasuvadu ucitadalli|

vaiShNavAcAryara mata uddhArakarta|

bhraShTavAdigaLannu nuggalotti|

kRuShNavaMdita namma vijaya viThalarEyana|

aShTakatRutva sthApisida dhIrA ||

aTTAtALa

---------

kusharAya illi tapasu mADidaMtha|

vasatiya nODi digdEshava jayisi mA|

sadalli tiLidu akShObhyatIrtharu illi|

nasunagutale vAsavAdaru biDidale|

RuShikulOttamarAda jayarAyaru nitya|

besane besane baMdu guptadalli pU |

jisuvaru prItile EneMbenAcarya shashivarNadaMte

poLeva darushana graMtha |

rasa pUritavAgi vistarisidaru vi|

kasitava mADi karada kannaDiyeMte|

kushala mAnavarige ~g~jAna heccuvaMte|

vasudheyoLage namma vijaya viThalarEya|

vashavAguvadakke prasAda mADidaru||

AditALa

-------

I munivolidare avane bhAgyavaMta|

bhUmiyoLage mukti yOgyanenisuvanu|

bhIma bhavAMbudhi batti pOguvadu ni|

ssImanAguva paMca bhEdhArtha pramEyadalli|

sUsutalipparu svAmi Igale baMdu duruLa manujana nODi nA|

koTTu kRutArthanna mADu|

rAma suguNadhAma vijayaviThalarEyanna|

nAma koMDADuva TIkAcArya||
